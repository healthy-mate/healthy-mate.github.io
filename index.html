Literature Survey

Heart failure is a prevalent chronic condition, and the healthcare industry is increasingly turning to AI assistants to improve patient management. Recent advancements in AI and natural language processing have shown the effectiveness of AI assistants in enhancing medication adherence, appointment scheduling, and dietary control. This study aims to design an AI assistant tailored for heart failure patients to provide medication reminders, appointment scheduling, and diet planning. The potential benefits of AI-based interventions in managing heart failure could lead to improved patient outcomes. Furthermore, personalized recommendations focusing on lifestyle factors, such as weight management, lipid profile, physical activity, and smoking cessation, have proven beneficial for cardiac patients. Psychological factors like anxiety and depression are also linked to heart disease, with meditation, especially Transcendental Meditation (TM), showing positive effects on cardiovascular health. Machine learning and data analytics have been applied to predict and manage heart disease, utilizing various algorithms like Artificial Neural Networks, Decision Trees, and Support Vector Machines. Additionally, IoT and machine learning-based health monitoring systems have been developed to collect vital signs data and predict the risk of heart attacks. Deep learning techniques, including Recurrent Neural Networks, have shown promise in improving disease prediction accuracy. These methods highlight the potential of machine learning and data analytics in predicting and managing heart disease.

In summary, the comprehensive approach to heart failure management involves four key components:

ECG Analysis and Machine Learning:Utilizing ECG records with smart devices and machine learning algorithms for real-time heart health analysis.

Smart Device for Early Detection: A smart device measuring vital body factors, enabling early detection of heart failure threats.

Personalized Lifestyle Recommendations: Tailored lifestyle recommendations for weight management, lipid profile, physical activity, and smoking cessation.

Patient Support and Diet Assistance: Using a chatbot for patient support and appointment scheduling, combined with a customized diet recommendation system, promotes better care and adherence.


Research Problem

A meaningful research problem involves investigating the development and implementation of an integrated home-based healthcare technology system tailored to heart patients. This research will focus on remote health measurements and care management, addressing the challenges of at-home care for these patients. By harnessing wearable sensors, IoT devices, and advanced software applications, the study aims to streamline the monitoring and management of critical health factors like blood pressure, heart rate, and cholesterol levels. Furthermore, it seeks to provide personalized guidance on diet, exercise, and stress reduction, optimizing the physical and mental well-being of heart patients. This research aims to enhance the quality of life and overall outcomes for cardiac patients in a home-based care setting.

Research Gap

Integration of AI and Psychological Support:

The existing research focuses on AI-driven interventions for physical aspects of heart disease management. However, there's a gap in investigating the integration of AI with psychological support to address the impact of anxiety and depression on heart disease.

Long-Term Efficacy and Patient Adoption:

Research has shown the potential benefits of AI interventions, but there's a gap in long-term efficacy studies and understanding patient adoption rates. Exploring how patients sustain engagement and adhere to AI-assisted strategies is essential.

Health Equity and Access:

Many AI-driven solutions are designed for tech-savvy individuals, leaving out populations with limited access to the internet. Research gaps exist in addressing health equity. Ensuring these innovations are accessible to all segments of the population.

Research objectives

Measure body factors using smart device and predict heart failures using trained ML algorithms.

To provide a personalized lifestyle activity recommendation system for physical and mental wellbeing of heart patients.

To give personalized and immediate support with heart failure concerns, arranging appointments, and medication adherence, which ultimately improves outcomes for patients and health.

Methodology

1) User need to get ECG record of the patient and need to find predicted ECG is normal or Abnormal. If the ECG is abnormal, that means patient has a cardiac issue.

2) To clarify patient has a Heart attack risk, User need to get patient’s BPM, body temperature and blood oxygen level.

3) After getting those things and user need to answering the questionnaire. Then the system generated the actual heart attack risk of the patient.

4) If the patient has High or Medium heart attack risk, system gives the recommendations.

5) AI assistant to communicate with patients, remind them to take medication, schedule doctor appointments, and create personalized meal plans for heart patients based on their calorie intake.
